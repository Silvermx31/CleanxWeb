Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I get paid?

The CleanX payment cycle starts on Monday at 00:00 and ends on Sunday at 23:59. Payments are sent to your bank account weekly — no need to wait until the end of the month.

How much can I earn on the CleanX platform?

Your income depends on how many cleaning jobs you complete. Higher demand periods, tips, and promotions can significantly increase your earnings.

Where can I track my earnings?

You can monitor your earnings directly in the CleanX app. Open the main menu and select "All Jobs" to view detailed information about your income, bonuses, and tips.

Do I have to follow a fixed schedule?

No! You operate as an independent partner and choose when and how much you want to work. There are no minimum or maximum working hours.

What are the requirements to become a CleanX partner?

You must be at least 18 years old, possess a photo ID, and have a smartphone with an Estonian phone number. Detailed requirements are listed in the registration form.

Why should I join CleanX?

CleanX offers a flexible and fast way to earn money through cleaning tasks. Registration is quick and easy, payments are made weekly, and there are no monthly fees.

When can I start working?

Simply complete the registration form! We will review your information and send you a confirmation with the next steps as soon as possible.

How much work will I get?

The number of job offers depends on your location and the time period. The CleanX platform is optimized to offer as many work opportunities as possible to partners.

What kind of support do I get?

CleanX provides 24/7 customer support in case of problems and offers training materials and recommendations to make your work as easy and convenient as possible.
