Who Are We?

CleanX is an innovative platform that solves one of the biggest problems for car owners in winter — removing snow and ice from vehicles. Our goal is to make car snow removal fast, convenient, and accessible for everyone. We are a passionate and ambitious team developing a modern technological solution to connect car owners and independent service providers through an easy-to-use app.

What Do We Offer?

With the CleanX app, you can:

  • Order snow removal services for your car quickly and easily.
  • Offer services and earn extra income — you choose when and where you work.
  • Track service progress in real time: follow the cleaner's route and work status directly on the map.
  • Pay securely with debit and credit cards via the Stripe payment system.

The platform is designed to be flexible, secure, and user-friendly, providing convenience to both clients and service providers.

How to Earn with CleanX?

If you want to earn extra income as a snow cleaner:

  • Register on the CleanX platform.
  • Accept orders at your chosen time and area.
  • Earn 80% of each job’s order value — CleanX only takes a 20% commission.
  • You can work as a sole proprietor, a limited company, or as a private individual, depending on your preferences and income level.

This way, we offer you the opportunity to earn extra money, manage your own time, and work where the need is the greatest.
